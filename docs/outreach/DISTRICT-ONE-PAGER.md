# Bonsai Instant Citation

**A browser citation tool for students that collects nothing.**

---

## What it is

Bonsai is a browser extension that builds correct MLA, APA, Chicago, and IEEE citations —
in-text and full — in a couple of clicks, right from the page a student is already reading. No
retyping source details into a form, no separate citation-generator site, no ads.

## Why it's safe — COPPA / FERPA, zero collection

Every citation is generated **on the student's own device**. There are no student accounts, no
sign-in, and no network requests made by the extension — nothing about a student or their
browsing is collected, transmitted, or stored anywhere. Because there is no data collection
event to govern, Bonsai typically clears a COPPA/FERPA privacy review quickly: there is nothing
for a district's counsel to find.

## How it deploys — one push, every campus

Bonsai installs through **Google Admin as a force-install extension** — a single push from
central IT rolls it out to every managed Chromebook or Chrome profile across a campus, or across
an entire network at once. Typical setup is **one afternoon** with your IT team; no per-device
rollout, no student-facing setup, no help-desk tickets.
