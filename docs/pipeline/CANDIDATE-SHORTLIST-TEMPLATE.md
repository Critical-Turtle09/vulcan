2. Score the five rubric dimensions (0–3) → sum = **ICP** (max 15). Band: PRIORITY 12–15 · QUALIFIED 8–11 · PARK ≤7.

## Field key

- **INSTITUTION** — school / network / district (you fill).
- **ARCHETYPE** — librarian · English-head · charter-director · writing-center · AP/IB-coord.

- **ICP** — the 5-dimension score.

- **STATUS** — `PARK` / `TO CONTACT` / `DRAFTED` / `SENT` / `REPLIED` / `PILOTING`.

## Sourcing lanes (how to fill this ethically — warm first, public second)

No scraping. Populate rows from lanes you already have standing to work:

- **Warm intros** — anyone in your network at a school/network/district. A warm path beats any cold score.
- **Inbound / existing interest** — anyone who has already asked about Bonsai.
- **Public professional directories** — district/network staff pages, conference attendee/speaker
  lists, professional-association member rosters (AASL librarians, NCTE English teachers). Record
  the **role and institution**; add a personal email only when the person has published it for
  professional contact, and only when you intend to reach out yourself.
- **Never**: bulk-harvest student or minor data, buy lists, or auto-send. VULCAN holds anything
  that would leave the machine until you say go.
